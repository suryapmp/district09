About District 9

  • Our story is quite unique. We wanted to create an experience of having food rather than just having a place for dining, and we have created two restaurants with a different feel from the time you walk in till the time you step out.
  • We want you to remember every moment you spend here. Hence, we have tried to keep the ambience subtle to help you immerse yourself creating a beautiful time with family and friends.
  • District 9, Mysuru branch is known for its ambience and tasty food, helping our customers create a family and friends experience. Whereas our District 17, Davangere branch is famous among travellers, friends groups and working people to spend a quality time with a different experience.
